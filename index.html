Loyalty for consumers, reputation for creators.

1

Contributors Develop Original & Informational Content

Content providers (individuals, groups or organizations) create a broad range of information that may help network participants in making investment decisions in the cryptocurrency markets.

The content can take many forms from analyst & sector reports, all the way to code vulnerability & security assessments.

2

Pareto Network distributes the Content to token holders

The information is curated structured by the network, and then distributed to token holders in a prioritized sequence. An algorithm is used to determine the order in which Pareto token holders are presented with the information. The result of this algorithm is called the waterfall.

By ranking higher in the waterfall, a token holder can act on valuable information before people that follow after him, giving her a significant advantage over the rest.

3

Content creators are rewarded based on the quality of their information

Upon receiving the information, token holders vote on its quality. By pricing votes at acceptable levels & by creating high quality content, contributors earn in return Pareto Tokens which they can choose to sell on secondary markets in order to generate revenue.

Content providers are therefore incentivized to provide quality information to the network, as they can profit more from it than if they were to act on it alone.

Token holders are incentivized to vote on information, as it is one of the factors that affects their waterfall position alongside the number of Pareto tokens they own and the amount of time they have owned it for
